Problem Statement: Maps

In the University of South Carolina, there is no one good way to figure out where to get to your classes as an incoming student. While a map of the buildings is provided, students are left clueless as to where in the massive buildings their classrooms are. Our app aims to provide an interactive and simple way to help students better locate their classes.
